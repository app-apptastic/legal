Privacy Policy

App: Habitify · Developer: Apptastic
Last updated: 5 June 2026

Habitify is an offline habit tracker. We designed it to respect your privacy: the app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

What data the app stores

  • The habits you create and your completion history (checks, counts, timer durations, notes, reminder times).
  • Your app settings (theme, default reminder time, etc.).

All of this is stored only on your device (in the app's private storage). It never leaves your phone, and we have no servers and no account system.

Data we collect or share

None. Habitify has no analytics, no advertising, no trackers, and no network calls for your data. Nothing is uploaded.

Permissions and why they're used

  • Notifications — to show your habit reminders and timer status. Used locally on your device.
  • Exact alarms — so reminders arrive at the time you set.
  • Foreground service — to keep a habit's focus timer running accurately while the app is in the background.
  • Run at startup — to re-schedule your reminders after the device restarts.

These permissions are used only for the features above. No permission is used to collect or send your data.

Crash diagnostics

The app itself contains no crash-reporting or analytics SDK. If you have opted in to share usage and diagnostics with Google Play, Google may collect anonymous crash information on our behalf through Android's standard mechanisms. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy and can be controlled in your device settings. We do not receive any personally identifying information from it.

Children

Habitify is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.

Deleting your data

Because everything is stored locally, you can remove all data at any time by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
